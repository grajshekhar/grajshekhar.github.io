About

I am Prof. Gannavarpu Rajshekhar. I am jointly affiliated with the Department of Electrical Engineering and Center for Lasers and Photonics at Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India.

Education

  • PhD, Photonics (2009-2012), Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL), Switzerland

  • Master of Technology, Laser Technology (2006-2008), Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, India

  • Bachelor of Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (2001-2005), Government Engineering College Raipur (now NIT Raipur), India

Experience

  • Associate Professor (2018-present) Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Kanpur

  • Assistant Professor (2014-2018) Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Kanpur

  • Swiss National Science Foundation Postdoctoral Fellow (2013-2014) Beckman Institute, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, USA

  • Postdoctoral Researcher (2012-2013 ) Applied Computing and Mechanics Laboratory, EPFL, Switzerland

Research

Our goal is to develop novel optical coherent imaging techniques for precision metrology . We work on both the experimental and computational aspects of coherent imaging. For more details, visit the Optical Metrology and Imaging Laboratory (OMIL) at IIT Kanpur.
